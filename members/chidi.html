Prof Chidi Onuoha Magnus is a Development Economist, author, teacher and researcher with diverse experience public service and academia. His research interest span climate finance, MRV methodology and GHG inventory, green growth/ green economy, blue economy, climate change and environmental sustainability, eco innovation, natural capital and ecosystem services, renewable energy and energy efficiency standardization. He is a senior research member of the following institute: Green Economic Institute UK; African Technology and Policy Studies Network Kenya, International Institute for Technology and Economic Development Association Scotland, African Heritage Institution Enugu Nigeria, Governing Inclusive Green Growth Africa (GIGGA) a UK funded research network domiciled and coordinated by University of Reading, African Green Growth Development Forum, Calabar. Member of United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change, Roaster of Expert on Green House Gas Inventory (GHGI) Quality Assurance; Member of Nigeria Climate Change Response Programme (NCCRP) a European Union funded four year programme, where he chairs the Waste Sector Work Group; Member of Africa Union, Scientific Research and Innovation Council, where he is member of Resources Mobilization, sub Committee. Just recently he was part of the 3-man team of African consultants commissioned by Africa Technology Policy Studies, Network Kenya and Pan African Climate Justice Alliance that developed a framework for Nationally Determined Contribution (NDC) Implementation index as well as monitoring/tracking tools for Africa. The process of turning the index into an app is ongoing. He teaches economics at Gregory University Uturu Nigeria where he is also combines as the Director West Africa Green Economic Development Institute (WAGEDI) and Centre for Advanced Transformation for Development in Africa.
